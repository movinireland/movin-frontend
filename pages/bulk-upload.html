Bulk upload your listings

Add your whole portfolio in one go: fill the Excel template (one property per row, with dropdowns for the fixed fields) and zip the photos it references. We'll create every valid listing and attach its images automatically.

Agents only

Bulk upload is available to agent accounts. Create an agent account to use it.

Become an agent →

1 Download the Excel template

Each row is one property. Keep the header row. The template is an .xlsx with built-in dropdowns for the fixed-choice fields (listing type, property type, county, BER, plan and the yes/no options) — just pick from the list. The images column lists the photo filenames for that row, separated by semicolons (the first becomes the main photo).

listing_type: sale · rent · share  |  property_type: house · apartment · bungalow · site · office · retail · industrial · land · commercial  |  plan: standard · featured

2 Prepare your photos

Put all photos into a single .zip file. Filenames must match exactly what you put in the images column (e.g. rathmines-front.jpg). Supported: JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, HEIC. Photos are optional — rows without images still create a listing.

3 Upload

Up to 100 listings per upload. Listings follow normal pricing and go to review before publishing.
