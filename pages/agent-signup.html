Agent questions

Is it really free for agents right now?

Yes. Estate agents list free for 6 months from 25 May 2025 — that's until 25 November 2025. After that, standard listings are €29.99 each or €149.99/month for unlimited. We'll always give 30 days' notice before charging an active subscription differently.

How does bulk upload work?

Download our CSV template from your dashboard, fill one row per property, and reference your photo filenames. Zip those photos, then upload the CSV and ZIP together — we create every valid listing and attach its images automatically. Up to 100 listings per upload.

Can I list commercial property?

Absolutely. Agent accounts can list residential (houses, apartments, sites) and commercial (office, retail, industrial, land) in the same place.

Do you take commission on sales?

Never. Movin.ie is a flat-fee marketplace — you keep 100% of your commission. We only charge the listing fees shown above.

Can I cancel the subscription?

Any time. The Unlimited subscription cancels at the end of your current billing period, so you keep what you've already paid for.
